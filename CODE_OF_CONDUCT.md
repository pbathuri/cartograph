# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge
We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of
age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality,
personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insulting/derogatory comments, personal or political attacks,
publishing others' private information, and other conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers via a
private GitHub security advisory or by opening a confidential channel with the maintainers. Maintainers
will review and respond, and may take any action they deem appropriate, up to and including a ban.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1.
